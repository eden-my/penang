Medium-sized evergreen tree to 30 m tall. Leaves 7.5-12.5 cm long, narrowly obovate, golden-coppery below. Flowers c. 1 cm across, white, in axillary clusters. Fruits 15-17 mm long, oblong, brown ripening red.
