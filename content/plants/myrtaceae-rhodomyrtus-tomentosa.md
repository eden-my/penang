Evergreen shrub or treelet. Leaves 2.5-9 cm long, opposite, lanceolate to elliptic. Flowers in axillary clusters of 3, c. 1.7-2 cm across, pink, white. Fruits ellipsoid with persistent calyx, c. 15 mm long.
