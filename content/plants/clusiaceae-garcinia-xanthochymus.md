Small dioecious tree to 15 m tall. Leaves opposite, 12-24 cm long. Male and female flowers borne on separate plants, white. Fruits fleshy, subglobose with pointed tip that ends with a persistent stigma, up to 9 cm wide, ripening pale orange to dark yellow.
