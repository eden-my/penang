Evergreen shrub or small tree to 5 m tall. Leaves opposite, 1.5-8.2 cm long. Flowers in branched terminal clusters, white, pendulous, fragrant, 1-2.5 cm long. Fruits paired follicles, 8.5-17.5 cm long, linear.
